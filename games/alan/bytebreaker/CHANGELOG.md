# Changelog

## Player characters

Each rival's player character now renders beside their name in the live-scores
strip, so you can spot who's who at a glance.

## Initial release

Bytebreaker — a neon brick-breaker for the terminal, one cabinet per player.

- Continuous bit physics advanced against real elapsed time and sub-stepped, so a
  fast bit never tunnels a byte; paddle english steers your bounce, and every
  smash throws off sparks.
- Levels that climb: more rows, armoured bytes that take two hits, and a quicker
  bit on every cleared wall.
- Four powerups drop from shattered bytes and fall for the paddle to catch — wide
  paddle, multiball, slow bit, and an extra life.
- Three lives, a durable per-account high score posted to a shared leaderboard,
  and a multiplayer score race that shows each rival's live total.
