# Changelog

## v1 — first shift

Spaceterm launches: co-op bridge duty for 1–6 crew. Panels of technobabble
controls on single-key hotkeys (`w e r t` / `s d f g`), timed orders routed
mostly to *other people's* panels, a HAIL key for remote crews, a shared hull,
and warp-charge sectors that keep shrinking the timers. Four anomalies from
sector 2 — meteor storms, solar flares, wormhole transits, coolant leaks — and
a co-op leaderboard score: sectors cleared, banked identically by the whole
crew.
